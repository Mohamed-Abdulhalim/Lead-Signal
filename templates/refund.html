Our commitment

If LeadSignal does not work as described, we will make it right. We offer refunds on paid plans within 7 days of purchase — no lengthy disputes, no runaround.

1. What We Sell

LeadSignal sells one-time license keys that unlock higher export limits on lead data. These are digital products delivered instantly upon payment. No physical goods are shipped.

The free tier is available with no payment required. Purchases only apply to paid export plans.

2. Refund Window

You may request a full refund within 7 days of your purchase date, provided the conditions below are met. After 7 days, refunds are not available except in cases of duplicate charges or technical failures on our end.

3. Eligible & Non-Eligible Situations

Eligible for refund

Tool did not work after purchase

Your license key was not delivered, failed to activate, or the export feature did not function as described. Contact us and we will fix or refund immediately.

Eligible for refund

Duplicate charge

You were charged more than once for the same plan. We will refund the duplicate charge within 48 hours of your report.

Eligible for refund

Data quality significantly misrepresented

The data coverage or quality for your specific use case was materially different from what was described on the product page. Contact us with details within 7 days.

Not eligible

Change of mind after use

You downloaded a CSV export, used the data, and then decided you no longer need it. The product was delivered and used as intended.

Not eligible

Request made after 7 days

Refund requests submitted more than 7 days after the purchase date are not eligible, unless there is an ongoing technical failure.

Not eligible

No results for your specific niche

LeadSignal's database does not cover every category or location. Coverage limitations are disclosed on the product page. If results were zero, use the free tier to verify before purchasing.

4. How to Request a Refund

Email us at contact@halim.pro with:

  • Your order number or the email used at checkout.
  • A brief description of the issue.
  • Any relevant screenshots if the issue is technical.

We will respond within 48 hours (usually faster) and process eligible refunds within 5 business days back to your original payment method.

5. Payment Processors

Payments are processed by Gumroad or Paymob depending on your region. Refunds are issued through the same processor used for your original purchase. We do not store your card details.

Refund timing on your statement depends on your bank — typically 3 to 10 business days after we initiate the refund.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our product and payment setup evolves. The version in effect at the time of your purchase applies to your order. The "Last updated" date above reflects the current version.
