FAQ

Common questions

What data comes with each lead?
Name, category, phone(s), website, socials (when found), rating, reviews count, address, and a direct Google Maps profile link.
Is the free tier really free?
Yes — you can search and preview up to 10 results per search for free, no account required. Paid export plans with higher limits are coming soon. Enter your email in the request form to be notified when they launch.
What's the best way to search?
Use broad category names like dentist, cafe, or gym rather than brand names. For location, use City, Country format (e.g. Riyadh, Saudi Arabia) for best results.
Can I export to CSV?
Yes — after running a search, click "Preview & Export CSV" to choose your columns, apply deduplication, and download the file.
