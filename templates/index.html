What is LeadSignal?

LeadSignal is a Google Maps leads scraper built for professionals. It helps you find real businesses by category and city, then exports cleaned data including phones, websites, ratings, and addresses into a CSV you can plug into any CRM or dialer. Instead of manually copying from Google Maps, you get structured, ready-to-use leads in a few clicks.

How it works
Search
Choose a category and a location. Get only real businesses from Google Maps.
Clean
Phones normalized, websites fixed, socials extracted, photos deduped, all tied to Google Maps results.
Export
Download tidy CSVs you can use now in outreach, sales, or recruiting workflows.
FAQ
What data comes with each lead?
Name, category, phone(s), website, socials (when found), rating, reviews, address, Google Maps link, and a representative photo URL.
How clean is the output?
Phones normalized, websites un-shortened, social links parsed, photos deduped per row. CSV is ready for CRM or dialer import.
Do I need to sign up or add an email?
No. LeadSignal doesn't require accounts, emails, or logins. Search, preview, export, done.
