What is LeadSignal?

LeadSignal is a Google Maps leads scraper built for people who prefer data over copy-paste. Pick a category and a city, then export cleaned leads with phones, websites, ratings, addresses, and Maps links into a CSV you can drop into a CRM or dialer.

How it works
Search
Choose category + location. Get real businesses, not random noise.
Clean
Phones normalized, websites fixed, socials extracted when present.
Export
Download a tidy CSV ready for outreach workflows.
FAQ
What data comes with each lead?
Name, category, phone(s), website, socials (when found), rating, reviews, address, Google Maps link, and a representative photo URL.
How clean is the output?
Phones normalized, websites un-shortened, social links parsed, photos deduped per row. CSV is ready for CRM or dialer import.
Do I need to sign up or add an email?
No accounts, no email, no logins. Search, preview, export, done.
