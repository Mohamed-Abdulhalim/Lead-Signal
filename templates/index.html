FAQ

Common questions

What data comes with each lead?
Business name, category, phone number (cleaned), website, social links when available, rating, review count, full address, and a direct Google Maps profile link.
Is it free to try?
Yes — the tool is free to try.
What categories and locations work best?
Use broad category names like dentist, café, or gym rather than specific brand names. For location, use City, Country format — e.g. Riyadh, Saudi Arabia — for best results.
Can I filter before exporting?
Yes. You can filter by minimum rating, phone availability, website availability, and address keyword. On export, you can choose exactly which columns to include in your CSV.
Is this affiliated with Google?
No. LeadSignal uses publicly available business listing data. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Google in any way.
How should I use the data responsibly?
Use exported leads for legitimate outreach and research only. Respect local anti-spam laws and regulations in your jurisdiction. If consent-based outreach is required in your country, follow those rules.
